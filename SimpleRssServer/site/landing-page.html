The simplest RSS reader on the planet.

Provide one or more RSS feed URLs as query parameters:

rssrdr.com/?rss=https://example.com/feed.xml&rss=https://another.com/rss/

For example.

That's it.

It works on all platforms and browsers.

Want to synchronize your feeds across your devices? Just copy and bookmark the url.
